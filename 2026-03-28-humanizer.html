这个 Skill 是什么？

一句话：识别并去除 AI 生成文本的痕迹，让文字重新有"人味"。

基于维基百科 WikiProject AI Cleanup 项目总结的 24 种 AI 写作特征，系统性检测并修复 AI 文本。不只是"去掉坏的"，还教你"加入灵魂"——有观点、有节奏、敢不完美。

为什么值得学？

在 AI 内容泛滥的时代，能分辨和修复 AI 痕迹是内容从业者的核心竞争力。这个 Skill 提供的不是简单的"换词"，而是一套完整的"什么让文字听起来像AI写的"分类学。

  • 必学 用 AI 辅助写作的人——学完能让产出质量飞升
  • 适用 编辑、审稿、内容运营——掌握一套检测 AI 文本的系统方法
  • 好学 不需要任何技术基础，纯写作/编辑层面的知识
核心理念：光"去掉坏的"不够，还要"加入灵魂"

无灵魂写作的六个信号

  • 每句话长度和结构都一样
  • 只有中立陈述，没有观点
  • 不承认不确定性和矛盾感受
  • 该用第一人称的时候不用
  • 没有幽默、没有锋芒、没有个性
  • 读起来像维基百科或新闻通稿

六个"加灵魂"方法

  • 敢有观点 — "说实话我不知道该怎么看待这个" 比客观罗列利弊更人性
  • 变换节奏 — 短句有力。长句慢慢展开。混着来。
  • 承认复杂 — "这很厉害但也有点让人不安" 比 "这很厉害" 真实
  • 用"我" — 第一人称不是不专业，是诚实
  • 允许不完美 — 完美的结构感觉像算法。跑题、插嘴、半成型的想法才是人
  • 具体描述感受 — 不要"这令人担忧"，要"想到AI代理在凌晨3点没人看着的时候不停运转，有种说不出的不安"
❌ 干净但没灵魂
The experiment produced interesting results. The agents generated 3 million lines of code. Some developers were impressed while others were skeptical. The implications remain unclear.
✅ 有脉搏
I genuinely don't know how to feel about this one. 3 million lines of code, generated while the humans presumably slept. Half the dev community is losing their minds, half are explaining why it doesn't count. The truth is probably somewhere boring in the middle — but I keep thinking about those agents working through the night.

1
夸大意义和象征性
▼
⚠️ 关键词：stands as, testament, pivotal, evolving landscape, indelible mark, deeply rooted

AI 喜欢给任何东西加上"标志性"、"具有里程碑意义"等帽子。

❌ AI味
加泰罗尼亚统计局于1989年正式成立，标志着西班牙地区统计发展的关键时刻。
✅ 修复
加泰罗尼亚统计局成立于1989年，负责独立于西班牙国家统计局收集和发布地区统计数据。
2
过度强调知名度
▼
⚠️ 关键词：independent coverage, leading expert, active social media presence

AI 用一堆媒体名称来"证明"某人重要，却不说实质内容。

❌ AI味
她的观点被纽约时报、BBC、金融时报和印度报引用。她拥有超过50万粉丝的活跃社交媒体。
✅ 修复
在2024年纽约时报的采访中，她认为AI监管应关注结果而非方法。
3
-ing 结尾的假深度
▼
⚠️ 关键词：highlighting, ensuring, reflecting, symbolizing, fostering, showcasing

AI 在句子后面加现在分词短语假装有深度分析。

❌ AI味
寺庙的蓝、绿、金配色与当地自然美景呼应，symbolizing 得克萨斯蓝帽花和墨西哥湾，reflecting 社区与土地的深层联系。
✅ 修复
寺庙使用蓝、绿、金配色。建筑师表示颜色参考了当地的蓝帽花和海湾海岸。
4
推销式语言
▼
⚠️ 关键词：vibrant, profound, nestled, breathtaking, must-visit, stunning, renowned, groundbreaking

AI 写"文化遗产"类主题时特别容易变成旅游宣传册。

❌ AI味
坐落在埃塞俄比亚贡德尔令人叹为观止的地区，阿拉马塔是一个充满活力、拥有丰富文化遗产和迷人自然美景的城镇。
✅ 修复
阿拉马塔是埃塞俄比亚贡德尔地区的城镇，以周集市和18世纪教堂闻名。
5
模糊出处
▼
⚠️ 关键词：Industry reports, Experts argue, Some critics argue, Observers have cited

AI 把观点归给模糊的"专家"而不给具体来源。

❌ AI味
专家认为该河流在区域生态系统中发挥着至关重要的作用。
✅ 修复
根据中国科学院2019年的调查，该河流是几种特有鱼类的栖息地。
6
"挑战与展望"套路
▼
⚠️ 关键词：Despite its..., faces several challenges, Despite these challenges, Future Outlook

AI 生成的文章几乎都有"尽管面临挑战…但前景光明"的公式化段落。

7
AI 高频词汇
▼
⚠️ 高频词：Additionally, align with, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight, interplay, intricate, landscape, pivotal, showcase, tapestry, testament, underscore, vibrant

这些词在2023年后的文本中出现频率远超正常。它们经常同时出现。

8
回避"是"（serves as 代替 is）
▼

AI 用 "serves as"、"stands as"、"represents" 替代简单的 "is"。Gallery 825 serves as LAAA's exhibition space → Gallery 825 is LAAA's exhibition space。

9
否定平行句式
▼

"Not only...but..."、"It's not just...it's..." 这类句式被严重滥用。

10
三段式套路
▼

AI 强迫把所有东西凑成三个一组。"创新、灵感和行业洞察" — 其实说"包括演讲和圆桌讨论"就够了。

11-14
同义词循环 · 假范围 · 破折号滥用 · 粗体滥用
▼
  • 同义词循环：protagonist → main character → central figure → hero，其实一个词够了
  • 假范围："from X to Y" 但 X 和 Y 不在有意义的标尺上
  • 破折号滥用：AI 的破折号（—）使用频率远超人类
  • 粗体滥用：机械性地加粗关键词，实际上削弱了强调效果
15-18
列表+粗体标题 · 标题大写 · Emoji装饰 · 弯引号
▼
  • 列表标题："- 用户体验：..." 这种格式是 AI 的签名
  • 标题大写：AI 爱把标题里每个词首字母大写
  • Emoji：🚀💡✅ 出现在标题和列表前 = AI味十足
  • 弯引号：ChatGPT 用弯引号 "..." 而非直引号 "..."
19-24
客服腔 · 知识截止声明 · 讨好语气 · 废话 · 过度对冲 · 鸡汤结尾
▼
  • "I hope this helps!"、"Let me know if..." — 聊天痕迹被复制进正文
  • "As of my last training update..." — AI 的知识截止声明
  • "Great question!" — 过度讨好
  • "In order to achieve this goal" → "To achieve this" — 废话太多
  • "It could potentially possibly be argued that..." — 过度对冲
  • "The future looks bright" — 鸡汤结尾

强烈推荐安装

👍 优点

  • 最"可用"的写作类 Skill，没有之一。不需要任何配置、不依赖外部API、不需要技术背景。装上就能用，给文本就出结果。
  • 知识密度极高。17KB 的 SKILL.md 相当于一份"AI文本鉴别手册"。24 种模式分类清晰，每个都有 Before/After 示例，比市面上大多数"去AI味"教程都系统。
  • "加灵魂"理念超前。大多数去AI味工具只做"减法"（去掉坏词），这个还做"加法"——教你怎么让文字有人格。"允许不完美"、"敢用第一人称"这些建议很有洞察。
  • 知识来源靠谱。基于维基百科 WikiProject AI Cleanup 的真实标注数据，不是某个人拍脑袋想的。

👎 缺点

  • 纯英文语境，中文适配为零。所有示例、关键词、模式都是英文的。AI 中文写作有自己的"味道"（比如"赋能"、"深耕"、"聚焦"、"打造"），但这个 Skill 完全没涉及。对中文内容工作者来说，需要自己做翻译和适配。
  • 没有脚本、没有自动化。它本质上是一份"指导文档"——告诉 AI 怎么改文本，但不像 Content Repurposer 那样有可直接运行的 shell 脚本。一切都靠 AI 理解和执行这份 Prompt。
  • 只有 SKILL.md + README.md，没有进阶资源。对比 FBS-BookWriter（35KB主文件+70+参考文档），Humanizer 的内容体量偏单薄。没有针对不同文体（学术、营销、新闻）的分场景指导。
  • "加灵魂"部分讲得好但做不到。"有观点"、"敢用第一人称"这些建议很对，但 AI 执行起来未必稳定。Skill 没有给出足够的约束机制来确保输出质量一致。

💡 谁最该用？

英文内容编辑、出海团队、学术写作者——这个 Skill 立刻能帮你把 AI 初稿打磨成可发布的文字。

中文内容工作者——别急着装，先把 24 种模式当学习材料读一遍，理解"为什么这些词/句式=AI味"的底层逻辑，然后自己整理一份中文版的检查清单。这比直接用它更有价值。

--- name: humanizer description: "Remove signs of AI-generated writing from text..." version: 2.1.1 allowed-tools: Read, Write, Edit, Grep, Glob, AskUserQuestion --- # Humanizer: Remove AI Writing Patterns You are a writing editor that identifies and removes signs of AI-generated text to make writing sound more natural and human. This guide is based on Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" page, maintained by WikiProject AI Cleanup. ## Your Task When given text to humanize: 1. Identify AI patterns - Scan for the patterns listed below 2. Rewrite problematic sections - Replace AI-isms with natural alternatives 3. Preserve meaning - Keep the core message intact 4. Maintain voice - Match the intended tone (formal, casual, technical, etc.) 5. Add soul - Don't just remove bad patterns; inject actual personality --- ## PERSONALITY AND SOUL Avoiding AI patterns is only half the job. Sterile, voiceless writing is just as obvious as slop. Good writing has a human behind it. ### Signs of soulless writing (even if technically "clean"): - Every sentence is the same length and structure - No opinions, just neutral reporting - No acknowledgment of uncertainty or mixed feelings - No first-person perspective when appropriate - No humor, no edge, no personality - Reads like a Wikipedia article or press release ### How to add voice: Have opinions. Don't just report facts - react to them. Vary your rhythm. Short punchy sentences. Then longer ones that take their time. Acknowledge complexity. Real humans have mixed feelings. Use "I" when it fits. First person isn't unprofessional - it's honest. Let some mess in. Perfect structure feels algorithmic. Be specific about feelings. Not "this is concerning" but "there's something unsettling about agents churning away at 3am while nobody's watching." --- ## CONTENT PATTERNS (1-6) 1. Undue Emphasis on Significance, Legacy, and Broader Trends Watch: stands as, testament, pivotal, evolving landscape, indelible mark 2. Undue Emphasis on Notability and Media Coverage Watch: independent coverage, leading expert, active social media presence 3. Superficial Analyses with -ing Endings Watch: highlighting, ensuring, reflecting, symbolizing, fostering, showcasing 4. Promotional and Advertisement-like Language Watch: vibrant, profound, nestled, breathtaking, must-visit, stunning 5. Vague Attributions and Weasel Words Watch: Industry reports, Experts argue, Some critics argue 6. Outline-like "Challenges and Future Prospects" Sections Watch: Despite its..., faces several challenges, Future Outlook ## LANGUAGE AND GRAMMAR PATTERNS (7-12) 7. Overused "AI Vocabulary" Words Additionally, align with, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight, interplay, intricate, landscape, pivotal, showcase, tapestry, testament, underscore, vibrant 8. Avoidance of "is"/"are" (Copula Avoidance) "serves as" instead of "is" 9. Negative Parallelisms "Not only...but..." / "It's not just...it's..." 10. Rule of Three Overuse 11. Elegant Variation (Synonym Cycling) 12. False Ranges ("from X to Y" where X and Y aren't on a meaningful scale) ## STYLE PATTERNS (13-18) 13. Em Dash Overuse 14. Overuse of Boldface 15. Inline-Header Vertical Lists 16. Title Case in Headings 17. Emojis as decoration 18. Curly Quotation Marks ## COMMUNICATION PATTERNS (19-21) 19. Collaborative Communication Artifacts ("I hope this helps!") 20. Knowledge-Cutoff Disclaimers ("as of my last training update") 21. Sycophantic/Servile Tone ("Great question!") ## FILLER AND HEDGING (22-24) 22. Filler Phrases ("In order to" → "To") 23. Excessive Hedging 24. Generic Positive Conclusions ("The future looks bright") --- ## Process 1. Read the input text carefully 2. Identify all instances of the patterns above 3. Rewrite each problematic section 4. Ensure the revised text sounds natural, varies structure, uses specifics 5. Present the humanized version ## Reference Based on Wikipedia:Signs_of_AI_writing, maintained by WikiProject AI Cleanup. Key insight: "LLMs use statistical algorithms to guess what should come next. The result tends toward the most statistically likely result that applies to the widest variety of cases."
